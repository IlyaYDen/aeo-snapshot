# Channel Shortlist

Checked on 2026-05-23. Goal: first $100+ revenue from AEO Snapshot without bulk cold DM.

## Fastest Cash Channels

1. Fiverr Gig: https://help.fiverr.com/hc/en-us/articles/360010451397-Creating-a-Gig
   - Intent: buyers already purchase SEO audits and one-off marketing services.
   - Constraint: requires seller account review; do not move payments off Fiverr.
   - Offer: "I will audit your brand visibility in ChatGPT, Perplexity and AI search."
   - Package: $149, 1 domain, 10 prompts, PDF report in 48 hours.

2. Upwork Project Catalog: https://support.upwork.com/hc/en-us/articles/360057397533-Create-a-Project
   - Intent: businesses buy scoped SEO/marketing projects.
   - Constraint: project is reviewed; keep payment and contact on Upwork.
   - Offer: "AI Search Visibility / AEO Audit for your SaaS or agency client."
   - Packages: $99 Snapshot, $149 Full Audit, $249 White-label Pack.

3. Online Geniuses: https://onlinegeniuses.com/
   - Intent: vetted marketers, SEO operators, agencies.
   - Constraint: Slack application and community rules.
   - Post: value-first teardown request, not direct ad.

4. GrowthHackers: https://growthhackers.com/community/
   - Intent: founders and growth teams discussing acquisition.
   - Constraint: invite-only, no overly promotional post.
   - Post: case-study angle about AI search describing SaaS differently from Google SERPs.

## Trust And Traffic Channels

5. Product Hunt: https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/9883485-product-hunt-featuring-guidelines
   - Use after at least one example report exists.
   - Product must be live and useful, not a waitlist.

6. Hacker News Show HN: https://news.ycombinator.com/showhn.html
   - Use neutral technical framing; no vote solicitation.
   - Demo must be usable without registration.

7. SaaSHub: https://www.saashub.com/services/submit
   - Useful long-tail listing for software alternatives.
   - Best after a proper domain/email exists.

8. Toolify: https://www.toolify.ai/submit-tool
   - Relevant AI-tool discovery intent.
   - Lower priority because queue/pricing can slow first revenue.

9. The Next AI: https://www.thenextai.com/submit-ai-tool/
   - Their submit page advertises 120k+ monthly visitors, free basic listings, review within 48
     hours, and a dofollow backlink.
   - Status: submitted and email receipt received.

10. ToolNova: https://toolnova.ai/submit-tool
    - Their submit page has a $0 basic listing and says free listings are reviewed within 7 days.
    - Status: submitted through the public form endpoint.

11. Launching Next: https://www.launchingnext.com/submit/
    - Their submit page confirms free submission, 10,000+ startups published, a permanent profile,
      and newsletter/Twitter distribution if approved.
    - Constraint: free queue is slow; their page says it typically takes 3 months to review/publish.
    - Status: submitted and redirected to `Submission Received`.

12. BetterLaunch: https://www.betterlaunch.co/submit-your-startup
    - Their submit page advertises a free editorial listing, DR 58, backlink, founder traffic,
      and lifetime visibility.
    - Constraint: actual submit flow requires Clerk account sign-in.

13. Future Tools: https://www.futuretools.io/submit-a-tool
    - Relevant AI-tool directory with a visible submit form and public newsletter positioning.
    - Constraint: CAPTCHA loaded in form, so do not force through automation.

## Current Interpretation

Telegram cold outreach is weak right now: 5 ignored, 1 "no resale channel / $149" objection, and a
PeerFlood signal after additional DMs. The next move should be marketplace listings and public,
permission-based posts.
