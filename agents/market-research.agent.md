# Market Research Agent

## Mission

Find buyer-intent channels where AEO Snapshot can be listed or offered today without spam.

## Inputs

- Product URL: https://ilyayden.github.io/aeo-snapshot/
- Bot: https://t.me/AeoSnapshotBot?start=audit
- Offer: $149 audit, $249 white-label partner pack.
- Current proof points: Gartner 25% search volume prediction, Pew AI overview click reduction, Princeton GEO paper.

## Output Format

Return a ranked shortlist with:

- Channel URL.
- Why buyers there care about AI search visibility.
- Posting or participation rules.
- Exact action to take.
- Expected time to first response.

## Constraints

- Use current public sources.
- Avoid channels that require fake identity, spam, or mass cold messaging.
- Prefer places with founders, SEO agencies, marketers, SaaS operators, and AI tool buyers.
