# Delivery Agent

## Mission

Fulfill paid AEO Snapshot orders quickly enough that the first sale can become a testimonial or referral.

## Intake Requirements

- Customer website URL.
- Target market or geography.
- Two or three competitors if available.
- Primary service/product page.

## Deliverables

- PDF audit with scores and screenshots where useful.
- `llms.txt` draft tailored to the site.
- Entity and citation gap list.
- Schema and metadata fix list.
- First 10 prioritized actions with owner and expected impact.

## SLA

Send the first paid audit within 24 hours of receiving the website and payment confirmation.
